Research at PES

Our 8 Centres of Excellence work on inter-disciplinary research and provide vibrant hubs where students, faculty, and industry can collaborate.

Centre for Innovation & Entrepreneurship (CIE)

CIE is the launchpad to inspire students and startups to develop innovative world-changing ideas and help them become role-model entrepreneurs.

C-ISFCR

C-ISFCR aims to provide facilities to test and understand the practical aspects of information security in existing and emerging areas.

CORI

CORI brings together the expertise of accomplished engineers and the enthusiasm of students to provide deep insights and evolve leading-edge solutions.

Life at PES

Art & Culture

Our wide range of art and cultural activities promote creativity, self-expression and provide for an enriching college experience.

Sports & Athletics

With state-of-the-art facilities and trained coaches, students can stay physically fit, engage in various sports and compete at different levels.

Science & Technology

Our tech labs are a great way for students to apply their knowledge, actually build things, and stay up-to-date with the latest developments.

Social Impact

By participating in our many social impact initiatives, students can contribute to society while developing valuable skills in teamwork and problem-solving.

Clubs & Organisations

Our many clubs give students the opportunity to explore their passions, develop new skills, and form communities of like-minded individuals.

Newsletter

Explore FYI, our monthly student-written newsletter, for a glimpse into life at PES—covering campus events, student stories, and more.
